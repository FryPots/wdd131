Website Planning Document

Site Name

Crashout is a name for a web-comic publishing website, it originates from the term "Crashing out".
The name seeks to create an expectation for deeply emotional stories, diverse range of themes and topics in the catalog, and relatable and engaging experiences.

Site Purpose

Having a place where I am able to post my work aswell as my friend's; allow us to experiment in our work and the format; getting rid of the limitations other websites would not be ablet to provide.

Common Questions:

  • How can I post my own work?
  • Where can I vote for conceptual works to be approved?
